Tickydo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14.08.2025

At Tickydo Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information in our app.

Data Collection

Tickydo Pro does not store user data on centralized servers. All tasks and to-do list items are stored only on your device.

iCloud Backup

The app provides an iCloud backup option. Backups are made solely to your own iCloud account, and Tickydo Pro does not access this data.

Pro Version & In-App Purchase

The Pro version is activated only through In-App Purchase (IAP). Purchase transactions are processed by Apple.

No Sharing of Data

Since no personal information is collected centrally, Tickydo Pro does not share user data with third parties and does not track user behavior.

Third-Party Services

Tickydo Pro does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection.
