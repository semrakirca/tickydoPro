Tickydo Pro / Privacy Policy

Son Güncelleme / Last Updated: 14 Ağustos 2025

English

Tickydo Pro does not store user data on centralized servers. All tasks and to-do list items are stored only on your device.

The app provides an iCloud backup option. Backups are made solely to your own iCloud account, and Tickydo Pro does not access this data.

The Pro version is activated only through In-App Purchase (IAP). Purchase transactions are processed by Apple.

Tickydo Pro does not share user data with third parties and does not track user behavior.

Tickydo Pro does not use any third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertising.

The data stored on your device is protected by the device’s security features. We recommend using a device passcode or biometric authentication to prevent unauthorized access.
